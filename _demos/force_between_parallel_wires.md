### Description

Two wires are suspended vertically about 3 inches apart and in such a fashion that they are slightly loose. The wires are connected to a storage battery or high current DC supply either with current traveling in the same direction in the two wires or with the currents in opposite directions in the two wires. For the case when the currents are parallel, the wires will attract each other, and they will repel each other when the currents are antiparallel.
