### Description

A board is pounded randomly full of nails, spaced so that a ball bearing can roll between them. The balls are rolled through the maze of nails. Boards with different nail spacings may be used to show varying mean free paths. Different numbers of balls may be used to show different numbers of charge carriers. The board may be inclined at different angles to
illustrate the effects of applied EMF.
