### Description
A piece of soft iron wire is suspended between two supports. The tension is held constant by
means of a pulley and a weight. A horseshoe magnet is then used to pull the center of the wire upward by magnetic forces. Current is then passed through the wire until it becomes red hot and the wire loses its magnetic properties. The wire will fall free of the magnet.
