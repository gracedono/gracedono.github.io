### Description
A ball is held with an electromagnet and can be released by a micro-switch in the camera shutter release. A time exposure is made with the light produced by a stroboscope. If one
uses a Polaroid camera with transparent film, the picture may be developed and projected for presentation of the data to the class. A meter stick should be in the picture to establish the correct scale.
