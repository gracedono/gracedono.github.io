### Description

A set of brass rails is mounted on insulating supports and placed next to a large alnico magnet. The ends of the rails are connected to a galvanometer. A sliding bar is then moved along
the rails by the instructor. When the bar is moved in one direction, the galvanometer deflects one way, and when it is moved in the opposite direction, the galvanometer deflects oppositely.
