### Description
The monkey is a tin can held in place by an electromagnet and is released by a
microswitch in the barrel of the cannon. The cannon can be fired with various charges of compressed air. Before placing the projectile in the cannon, aiming is done with an optical system built into the cannon. The cannon is then loaded and fired. The professor should learn the switching arrangements carefully so he does not drop the monkey while loading the cannon.
