### Description

A rotatable disc has a large arrow on its periphery. This can be projected onto the wall with a point source lamp. The arrow on the disc can be oriented tangentially or radially, and one can nicely demonstrate the phase relations between displacement, velocity, and acceleration, showing how these quantities are related to the corresponding quantities in uniform circular motion.
