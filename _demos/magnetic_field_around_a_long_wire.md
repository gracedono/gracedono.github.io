### Description

A current-carrying wire is stretched across the lecture bench. A compass needle placed near the wire turns so it is at right angles to the wire. If the direction of the current is reversed, the direction of the needle also reverses. If the compass needle is moved from below the wire to above the wire, its direction will reverse. The right-hand rule can be established from the results of this demonstration.
