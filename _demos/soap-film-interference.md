### Description

A flat container with a black felt bottom has a lip across which one may support a
soap film. The film is illuminated with a carbon arc. Images are produced from reflections on the front and back surfaces of the film and are projected onto a screen with a projection lens. Since white light is used, the interference conditions are satisfied differently for different wavelengths, and the interference fringes are colored. The positions of the fringes change as the film evaporates.
