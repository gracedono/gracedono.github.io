### Description

A very heavy pendulum bob is supported from the ceiling on a vertical line about 6 feet from the blackboard. The instructor then places the back of his head against the board and the bob at the tip of his nose. He releases the bob from rest (with no initial velocity) and patiently awaits the return of the bob to the tip of his nose.
