### Description

A "mop" of insulating strings is fastened onto the end of an insulating rod. The strings repel each other when they are charged. If the strings are, say, negatively charged and a positive rod is held below, the strings will all curve inward at the bottom. If a negative rod is held below the strings, they will curve even further outward.
