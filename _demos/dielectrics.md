### Description

The apparatus is the same as for the previous demonstration. After charging the plates
are placed rather close together, allowing just enough room to insert slabs of dielectrics.
When the dielectric slabs are inserted between the charged plates, the electric displacement remains constant, and the electric field decreases. Since the distance between the plates is constant, the change in the electric field is proportional to the change in voltage.
