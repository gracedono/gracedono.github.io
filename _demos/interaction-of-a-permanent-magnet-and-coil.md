### Description
A solenoid is suspended by contacts in mercury cups so that it is free to move about a vertical axis. A bar magnet is mounted on a stand in such a way that the magnet can rotate about a vertical axis. The polarity of the magnet can be studied with respect to the current flow in the coil by showing the existence of repulsive and attractive forces.
