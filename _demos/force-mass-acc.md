### Description
An air cart carries an opaque flag 10 cm long, which can pass between a photodiode and a photo receiver to interrupt the beam of light. The time the light is off is measured on a fast counter. The falling weight attached to the cart is always made to fall one meter. The velocity measurement is made after the 1 meter of fall. The velocity acquired by the weight (and cart) for a combination of masses may be quickly determined.
