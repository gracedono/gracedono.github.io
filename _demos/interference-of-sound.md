### Description

A small speaker is connected to a tube, which splits the sound into two parts and subsequently
reunites it before the sound emerges from a horn. The lengths of the two paths can be varied by a type of slide trombone arrangement. When the path lengths of the sound are equal or differ by one wavelength, there is a strong sound emitted from the horn. If the path lengths differ by one-half wavelength, the emitted sound is very weak.
