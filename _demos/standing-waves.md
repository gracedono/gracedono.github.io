### Description

A DC motor is fitted with a small pulley that has a swivel at its periphery for attaching a piece of rope. The other end of the rope is supported by a stand with a swivel. With the rope running loosely over the pulley, the motor is adjusted to run at the speeds that will produce a varying number of nodes and loops on the rope. The motion can be slowed down by viewing the rope with a stroboscope.
